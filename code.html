# Welcome to the local-first editor

This is a privacy-first markdown editor that works entirely in your browser. All analysis and editing happens locally — no data is sent to external servers unless you explicitly enable AI evaluation.

## How it works

The editor provides real-time analysis including word counts, readability scoring, and complex sentence identification. To get started, simply paste in something you're working on and edit away.

If you see a red highlight, your sentence is so dense and complicated that your readers will get lost trying to follow its meandering, convoluted logic—try editing this sentence to remove the red highlight.

You can utilize a simpler word instead of a purple one. Passive voice and other weakening phrases are marked in blue.

## Features

  • Local Analysis: Instant feedback without internet connection
  • AI Evaluation: Optional AI-powered suggestions
  • Privacy First: Your content stays on your device
